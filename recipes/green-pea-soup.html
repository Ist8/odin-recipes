Description

Green pea soup with carrot and spätzle

Ingredients

  • 4 l water
  • 1 kg green peas
  • 2 carrots
  • 0.25 kg spätzle
  • 4 tbsp sunflower oil
  • 1 tbsp whole wheat flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 0.5 tbsp red pepper
  • 1 g parsley

Steps

  1. Roasting the carrots for 2 minutes
  2. Adding water, green peas, flour, red pepper, salt and sugar
  3. Cooking for 30 minutes
  4. Adding spätzle and parsley
  5. Cooking for 15 minutes
