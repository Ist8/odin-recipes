Description

Red bean soup with sour cream

Ingredients

  • 4 l water
  • 0.5 kg red beans
  • 0.5 l sour cream
  • 1 onion per portion
  • 0.5 tbsp salt
  • 0.25 tbsp black pepper
  • 0.25 tbsp red pepper
  • 2 bay leafs

Steps

  1. Soaking the beans for 1 hour in hot water
  2. Adding water, beans and bay leafs
  3. Cooking for 45 minutes
  4. Adding sour cream, salt, black pepper and red pepper
  5. Cooking for 15 minutes
  6. Adding one onion for every portion
